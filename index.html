Tutoring

My main focus is math olympiad and contest preparation. I work with motivated students who want to move beyond standard school math and develop stronger problem-solving skills.

Who I Work With

  • Students preparing for AMC 8, AMC 10, AMC 12, AIME, and similar contests
  • Students who are strong in school math and want to start competition math
  • Students who need a structured study plan instead of random problem solving
  • Students who want to improve accuracy, speed, and solution-writing

Class Structure

I usually meet with students once a week for a two-hour session. A typical class includes homework review, a short discussion of the main topic, guided problem solving, independent attempts, and follow-up practice. Most of the class is spent actively solving problems, not passively watching solutions.

Topics Covered

  • Algebra
  • Number theory
  • Combinatorics
  • Geometry
  • Sequences and series
  • Proof writing

Online Format

Lessons are online. I strongly recommend that students use a tablet or stylus so they can write during the session. Competition math tutoring works best when the student actively solves problems, writes solutions, and explains their thinking during class.
